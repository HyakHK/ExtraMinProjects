Energy Renewable

The energy sources that belong to this group are considered inexhaustible, as their quantities are constantly renewed when used.

Examples of renewable sources are: hydro (energy from water from rivers), solar (energy from the sun), wind (energy from the wind), biomass (energy from organic matter), geothermal (energy from the interior of the Earth) and oceanic (energy from tides and waves).

Some energy sources may vary in electricity generation as they need a proper placement to function effectively
