Renewable Energy Sources

Solar Energy

Posted on september 27, 2022 by Matheus mendes, Gabi Macedo, Arthur Alfaiate and Maria nyckolle
Energy Renewable

Solar energy is a renewable and sustainable source of energy that comes from electromagnetic radiation (light and heat) sent daily by the sun. This energy can be used by different technologies, such as solar heaters, photovoltaic panels and heliothermic plants.

In photovoltaic solar energy, when particles of energy from sunlight come into contact with solar panels, an electric current is generated that can be directed and used to power homes, businesses and industries, both in the city and in rural areas.

The energy from the sun is indispensable for the maintenance of life on Earth and serves as a catalyst for all thermal, dynamic and chemical processes, whether natural or artificially developed by man.
